{{SITE_NAME}} IM, our Instant Messaging bot, allows you to communicate with all your {{SITE_NAME}} contacts using your (XMPP based) instant messaging client. The supported services are: Google Talk, LiveJournal, and Jabber.

What you get

You get notified of posts, comments and Web feed updates from your contacts, and you can post your own messages and comments (even to channels!) right from your desktop.

Of course, sometimes you might want to not have all that going on in your Instant Messenger, so you can even stop the updates for a while (we don't mind, honestly - we know you actually need to work sometimes).

Activating

To get started, go to your IM client, and add {{IM_BOT}} to your buddy list. Then, send us an IM message containing the text (without the < and >): "SIGN IN <your {{SITE_NAME}} screen name> <your {{SITE_NAME}} password>"

After your account has been verified, you'll start receiving notifications from your contacts. It's that easy!

Communicating with the bot

If you want to stop receiving notifications, you can simply send 'STOP' via IM, and we'll stop.

To post to your stream, you just type in your message and hit Send. You'll get a notification (kinda like an echo) of your post, just so you know it's gone through as it should.

To comment on a post (or a comment), use the @ symbol to direct your comment to a user (example: "@lordi Rock on!") Doing this will add your comment to the most recent thread that person participated in.

If you want to add a new contact, you can FOLLOW [contact] ( e.g. "FOLLOW jyri"). If you want to remove a contact, you can LEAVE [contact].

Similarly, you can join and leave our channels - with JOIN or LEAVE (e.g. "JOIN #squid"). You can also post to channels by prefixing your message with #channelname ( e.g. "#squid Fresh squid fried with lime juice and garlic, served on a bed of crispy noodles = YUMMY!")
